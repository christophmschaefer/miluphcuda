1 # miluphcuda
2 
3 miluphcuda is a smoothed particle hydrodynamics code
4 
5 Check out the [documentation](https://christophmschaefer.github.io/miluphcuda/index.html)!
6 
7 * miluphcuda is the cuda port of the original miluph code.
8 * miluphcuda can be used to model fluids and solids.
9 * miluphcuda runs on single Nvidia GPUs with compute capability 5.0 and higher.
10 
11 
12 ## Main features
13 
14 * SPH hydro and solid
15 * self-gravity (via Barnes-Hut tree)
16 * porosity models (P-alpha, epsilon-alpha, Sirono)
17 
18 ## Hardware requirements
19 
20 miluphcuda runs on single NVIDIA GPUs using CUDA.
